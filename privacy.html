Privacy

Data use stays simple and transparent

LetterMeteor is a single-player, iPhone-first word game. The current app behavior does not include account creation, ads, payment processing, location permission, or cross-app tracking.

Support Page Send Privacy Question

Data Collection

Personal account data The current build has no account creation, sign-in, or personal profile flow.
Tracking The app does not use cross-app tracking, ad targeting, or data broker sharing.
Analytics data Usage signals such as app launches, session duration, screen and feature interactions, mode selection, goal progress, store, sharing, and leaderboard interactions may be measured with Firebase Analytics.

App Behavior

Local gameplay data Score, coins, level, streak, goal progress, and in-game statistics are stored locally on device.
Word validation Word checks are performed on device with bundled word lists. English UI uses the English list, and Turkish UI uses the Turkish list; online validation is not required for gameplay.
Data areas not used The app does not include flows to collect location, contacts, camera, microphone, photo library, payment information, or purchase history.

Third-Party Services And Updates

Firebase Analytics The current build uses Firebase Analytics. Firebase may process data such as app-instance identifiers, device and operating system information, session data, and product interaction signals for analytics and product quality.
Ads and external sign-in The current build does not use AdMob, ad networks, third-party sign-in, Firebase Auth, Crashlytics, Remote Config, or Messaging flows.
Contact Support and privacy buttons open the mail app with prepared subject lines.

This policy should be checked against the shipped production behavior and App Store Connect privacy answers.
